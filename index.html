Weekly Vehicle Inspection

Complete this checklist before your work week begins. Add all 9 required photos and submit to the fleet team. Takes about 5 minutes.

0 of 9 photos added

Exterior

Tires — driver side

Photo of both driver-side tires showing tread depth and sidewall. No bulges, cracking, or low pressure.

Tires — passenger side

Photo of both passenger-side tires. Check for even wear and adequate tread, including the rear tire.

Headlights & front lights

Front of vehicle with lights on — headlights and turn signals. All must be working and unobstructed.

Tail lights & brake lights

Rear of vehicle with brake lights activated. Show both tail lights clearly and any reverse lights.

Windshield & wipers

Full windshield photo — no cracks, chips, or obstructions. Include wiper blades so their condition is visible.

Body damage check

Walk-around photo showing all sides of the vehicle. If there is any new damage, capture a close-up of each area.

Under the hood, interior & documents

Fluid levels

Photo of the oil dipstick pulled out and wiped, showing the current oil level. Level should be between the min and max marks.

Dashboard & driver's seat

Photo from the driver's seat showing the full dashboard — no warning or check engine lights illuminated. Seat and seatbelt should be in good condition.

Registration & insurance

Photo of the current vehicle registration and proof of insurance together. Both must be present in the vehicle and up to date.
