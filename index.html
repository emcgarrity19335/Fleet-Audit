Weekly Vehicle Inspection

Complete this checklist before your work week begins. Add at least 1 photo per section (up to 4) and submit to the fleet team. Takes about 5 minutes.

0 of 9 sections complete

Exterior

Tires — driver side

Both driver-side tires showing tread depth and sidewall. No bulges, cracking, or low pressure.

Tires — passenger side

Both passenger-side tires. Check for even wear and adequate tread, including the rear tire.

Headlights & front lights

Front of vehicle with lights on — headlights and turn signals. All must be working and unobstructed.

Tail lights & brake lights

Rear of vehicle with brake lights activated. Show both tail lights clearly and any reverse lights.

Windshield & wipers

Full windshield — no cracks, chips, or obstructions. Include wiper blades so their condition is visible.

Body damage check

Walk-around photo showing all sides of the vehicle. Capture a close-up of any new damage.

Under the hood, interior & documents

Fluid levels

Oil dipstick pulled out and wiped, showing the current level between the min and max marks.

Dashboard & driver's seat

Full dashboard — no warning or check engine lights on. Seat and seatbelt in good condition.

Registration & insurance

Current vehicle registration and proof of insurance together. Both must be present and up to date.
